Amuses Me

by Cody Eaton

The three sites that I have put on this page are the three that "amuse me" day in and day out. My love for each is based on my gearhead soul. One being a magazine and the other two are brand sites for semi-trucks and car/pickups.

Goals/Ideas for Semi-trucks

This site is how I can see new ideas that other truck lovers have done to their own. This site shows the individual truck show winners that have happened each month. Also has history articles, giving the stories of old time truckers.
